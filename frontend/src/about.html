Why Stormalytics?

Field and court storming in college sports have become an epidemic. It seems like every upset, buzzer beater, and important win shown on SportsCenter ends with fans on the field of play.

The conference fines are an annoyance for the schools, but until it turns into something that affects the people doing the storming, the decades of storming propaganda will keep the bar for violating the sacred boundary between player and fan low.

However, there is hope. Research shows that the indoctrination paid for by Big Storm can be undone with a simple, objective system to help fans decide whether to head for the field or the exits.
